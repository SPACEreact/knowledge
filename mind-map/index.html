Execution Domains

The Five Pillars

Each domain has its own question. Each question reveals whether you're thinking or just vibing.

◆

01 · Design

"If this never moved, would it still work?"

Composition, hierarchy, typography, color psychology, negative space, grid systems, shape language.
◎

02 · Cinematography

"Does this feel physically real?"

Light physics, color temperature, focal length, exposure, camera movement, depth of field.
◌

03 · Sound

"Would this feel real with eyes closed?"

Production sound, room tone, foley, layering, diegetic/non-diegetic, silence, EQ & compression.
▤

04 · Editing

"What does this sequence say?"

Kuleshov effect, cause & effect, the blink, rhythm & tension, Eisenstein's montage methods.
↻

05 · Motion

"Does this feel alive?"

12 principles of animation, weight & physics, speed graphs, motion blur, follow-through, parallax. Even stylized motion must have weight, cause, and reaction.
