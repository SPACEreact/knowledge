The Ad Story Engine

This is the ad version of the harmonic cycle. Every effective ad runs this engine.

Desire

What the audience wants before the product appears: Status, Ease, Safety, Belonging, Pleasure, Control.
If desire is unclear, the product feels random.

Friction

Something blocks the desire: Effort, Cost, Confusion, Time, Fear, Inconvenience.
Without friction, there is no reason to act.

Relief

The product reframes or removes friction. It doesn't "save the world" â€” it changes the equation.
Relief must feel earned, not magical.
